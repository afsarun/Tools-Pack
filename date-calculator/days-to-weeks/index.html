About Days to Weeks Conversion

One week equals 7 days. To convert days to weeks, divide the number of days by 7. To convert weeks to days, multiply the number of weeks by 7. This conversion is commonly used in project management, pregnancy tracking (40 weeks), academic planning, and various scheduling applications.
