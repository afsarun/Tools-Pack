How to use

  1. Start typing in the box — it auto-saves after you pause.
  2. Click Export .txt to download your notes as a text file.
  3. Use Import to load a .txt file back into the editor.
  4. Click Clear to remove everything (you’ll be asked to confirm).

Tips

  • Works offline once loaded.
  • Avoid storing sensitive info—localStorage is not encrypted.
  • If your note is huge, consider splitting files (localStorage is ~5 MB per site).

FAQ

Where are my notes stored?

On this device only, inside your browser’s localStorage. Nothing is uploaded.

Is there a size limit?

Yes. Browsers typically allow around 5 MB per site for localStorage.

Can I sync across devices?

Not automatically. Use Export to save a .txt and Import it elsewhere.

Are notes encrypted?

No. Keep your device secure and avoid highly sensitive information.

Does it work offline?

Yes. After the first load, it works without internet.
