Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. It is widely used as a screening tool to identify potential weight problems.

Limitations of BMI

While BMI is a useful screening tool, it does have limitations. It may not accurately reflect body fat percentage in athletes with high muscle mass, older adults, or certain ethnic groups. Always consult with a healthcare provider for a comprehensive health assessment.
