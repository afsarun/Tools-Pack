How to Use the Stopwatch and Countdown Timer

Stopwatch

  1. Click Start to begin timing immediately.
  2. Click Pause to pause the stopwatch, and click Start again to continue.
  3. Click Reset to clear the time back to 00:00.000.
  4. Keyboard shortcuts: press Space to toggle Start/Pause, and R to reset.

Countdown Timer

  1. Enter the number of minutes and seconds you want to count down.
  2. Click Start Countdown (or press Enter) to begin.
  3. When the timer reaches 00:00, you will hear a short beep sound.
  4. Click Stop (or press Esc) if you want to cancel the countdown early.

Helpful Tips

  • If the beep doesn’t play, click anywhere on the page once to unlock audio (most browsers block sound until user interaction).
  • The stopwatch and countdown remain accurate even if you switch tabs—timing is based on monotonic time, not the animation frame rate.
  • This tool works offline once loaded, and no data ever leaves your device.
