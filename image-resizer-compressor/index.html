How to use

  1. Click Upload and choose an image (JPG/PNG/WebP).
  2. Enter new W/H. Keep Keep aspect on for proportional resize.
  3. Select Format (JPEG/PNG/WebP). For smaller files, prefer JPEG/WebP.
  4. If JPEG/WebP, adjust Quality (lower = smaller file).
  5. Click Resize & Download to save the result.

Tips

  • PNG ignores Quality. Use JPEG/WebP to compress more.
  • Large downsizing gives the biggest size reduction.
  • This tool runs locally—great for privacy and speed.

FAQ

How do I reduce image file size?

Downscale width/height and use JPEG/WebP with a lower Quality setting, then download.

What formats are supported?

Export as JPEG, PNG, or WebP. Quality applies only to JPEG/WebP.

Does this remove EXIF/metadata?

Yes. Canvas export typically strips EXIF/metadata.

Is my image uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything happens in your browser; nothing is sent to a server.

Why is the Quality slider disabled for PNG?

PNG is lossless, so there’s no quality/size trade-off exposed via the slider.
