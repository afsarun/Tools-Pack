How to use

  1. Pick a calculator tab: A% of B, What % is A of B?, Percent Change, or After % Increase/Decrease.
  2. Enter numbers only (no “%” symbol). Decimals are allowed.
  3. Results update as you type or when you click Calculate.
  4. Use Copy to copy the result; use Clear to reset fields.
  5. For “After % Increase/Decrease”, choose Increase or Decrease first.

Tips

  • Percent change is positive for increases and negative for decreases.
  • If a denominator is 0, the calculation is undefined.
  • Up to 8 decimal places are supported; trailing zeros are trimmed.
