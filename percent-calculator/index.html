Percentage Basics: Quick Reference

The word "percent" means "per hundred." So 25% = 25/100 = 0.25.

Basic formula to find X% of Y:
Result = (X ÷ 100) × Y

Basic formula for "what percentage A is of B":
Percentage = (A ÷ B) × 100

Real-World Examples

• What is 20% of 150? → 30

• 85 out of 200 is what percent? → 42.5%

• 12.5% of 800? → 100

• 33 out of 120 is what percent? → 27.5%
