How to Use the Text to PDF Converter

  1. Paste or type your text into the editor box (full Unicode supported, including Bangla, emoji, and special characters).
  2. Select your preferred Page Size, Orientation, and Margins for the PDF.
  3. Adjust the Font Size (points). This will apply to both the on-screen preview and the final PDF document.
  4. Optionally enable Header and/or Footer. The header shows the title at the top, while the footer displays the generated time at the bottom of each page.
  5. Click Preview & Print, then choose Save as PDF in your browser’s print dialog.

Helpful Tips

  • A4 is the standard in Bangladesh and Europe, while Letter is commonly used in the United States.
  • If your browser automatically adds extra page numbers or a URL, disable “Headers and footers” in the print settings.
  • This PDF tool runs 100% locally in your browser. Your text is never uploaded or stored on a server.
  • Once loaded, it also works offline without an internet connection.
