🎯 Output & Precision

  • Rounding modes: fixed decimals or significant figures

  • Exact ratios shown when clean (e.g., 12 in → 1 ft)
  • Linear: y = x × factor
  • Affine: y = (x + offset) × factor (°C ↔ K, °F)
