What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator Online is a free tool to calculate your exact age in years, months, and days from your Date of Birth (DOB). It also shows total days lived, your chronological age, and a next-birthday countdown. Use it to answer common questions like what is my age today, how many days until my birthday, or how old was I in 2018. This calculator works fully offline in your browser—no data leaves your device.

  • What is my age today? Keep “As of” as today and press Calculate.
  • How many days until my birthday? See the live countdown under the result.
  • How old was I on a past date (e.g., 2018-01-01)? Set that date in “As of”.
  • How many days is 11 years? Check “Total days lived” (leap years included).

How to use

  1. Select your Date of Birth (DOB). Use the calendar button to open the native picker.
  2. As of defaults to today—change it if you want age on another date (past or future).
  3. Click Calculate (or press Enter) to see years, months, days and total days lived.
  4. Use Copy to copy the result, or Reset to clear and start over.

Tips

  • Calendar buttons use the browser’s native date picker; if unsupported, the field simply focuses.
  • Leap-day birthdays (Feb 29) are handled: in non-leap years the next birthday is observed on Feb 28.
  • “As of” can be a future date to see how old you’ll be on that day.
  • Works offline once loaded; no data leaves your browser.
