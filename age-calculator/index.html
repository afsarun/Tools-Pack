The Mathematics of Age Calculation

How does our Age Calculator work? It uses a precise algorithm based on the Gregorian calendar to ensure accuracy. The process involves subtracting your birth year from the year and then adjusting for months and days to provide your exact age.

Handling Leap Years and Calendar Anomalies

A common question is, "How does an age calculator handle leap years?" Our algorithm is programmed to account for the extra day in February. This ensures that your age calculation remains perfectly accurate, even if you were born on February 29th. This is essential for a truly accurate age calculator.
