What is a Chronological Age Calculator?

A chronological age calculator is a precise tool used to determine an individual's exact age in years, months, and days. Unlike a standard age counter, this calculation is frequently used in educational assessments, medical records, and psychometric testing to establish a baseline for development.

How to Calculate Chronological Age

To perform the calculation manually, you subtract the birth date from the test date (or current date). The formula follows a specific order:

  1. Subtract the Days. (If the test day is smaller, borrow 30 days from the month).
  2. Subtract the Months. (If the test month is smaller, borrow 12 months from the year).
  3. Subtract the Years.
