Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Tools Pack is a collection of client-side utility tools. Most tools run directly in your browser; we do not store your inputs on our server.

What we collect

  • No account required.
  • LocalStorage data: some tools (e.g., Notes) use your browser’s localStorage. This stays on your device.
  • Diagnostics/analytics: to improve the site we may measure anonymized, aggregate metrics like page views or device type— not personally identifiable information.

Advertising

We may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads (personalized or non-personalized).

Learn how Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Cookies & your controls

  • Manage or clear cookies/site data from your browser settings.
  • You can clear any localStorage data from your browser.
  • Control ad personalization in your Google account settings.

Children’s privacy

Our site is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when needed; any new version will be published on this page.
