Percentage Calculator
A% of B, what percent is A of B, percent change, and after % increase/decrease.

A% of B

Formula: (A ÷ 100) × B

What percent is A of B?

Formula: (A ÷ B) × 100

Percent Change

Formula: ((New − Old) ÷ Old) × 100

After % Increase/Decrease

Formula: B × (1 ± A ÷ 100)
Tip: values update live as you type. Use the Copy button to copy the result.
